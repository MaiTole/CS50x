Team Little Italy

Our story started on a warm, dark evening when a college student found herself in tears over assignments and readings. Under a tight budget, a tired brain and worn body found comfort in the taste of home - a hearty, hot marinara tasted right off the wooden spatula before sprinkling just the right amount of oregano.

With recipes developed under Nonna's supervision, we strive to bring you the taste of the traditional Italian kitchen.
